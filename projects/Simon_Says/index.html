How to Play

Simon Says is a memory game where you have to follow the pattern of colors that the game shows you.

  • The game will display a sequence of colors, starting with one and increasing as you progress through levels.
  • Watch the sequence carefully, and when it's your turn, click the buttons in the exact order of the colors shown.
  • If you get the sequence correct, you will move to the next level, and a new color will be added to the sequence.
  • If you make a mistake, the game is over and you can start again by pressing the start button.
  • Your score increases based on your level progression.
